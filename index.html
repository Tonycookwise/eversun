Quality Assurance

Rigorous Control, Consistent Excellence

Our full-chain quality management system delivers 99.2% pass rate and less than 0.5% defect rate.

🔍

IQC · Incoming Quality Control

All raw materials inspected upon arrival. Silicone, stainless steel, plastics, and coatings tested for composition, thickness, and food-grade compliance before production.

⚙️

IPQC · In-Process Quality Control

Real-time monitoring during each production stage — molding, assembly, welding, coating. Random sampling at every workstation to catch defects before they reach the next process.

✅

FQC · Final Quality Control

AQL 2.5 / 4.0 sampling before shipment. Function, appearance, packaging, and labeling checks. Third-party testing by SGS, BV, or Intertek available upon request with full lab reports.
